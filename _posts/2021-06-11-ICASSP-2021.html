I presented our paper Light field style transfer with local angular consistency at the 2021 IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech and Signal Processing (ICASSP 2021). Check out this blog post for more.

ICASSP 2021 was my first ever academic conference. The conference was originally supposed to be held in Toronto, Canada but due to the pandemic it was held virtually. This meant I had to swap a first ever trip to Canada for Zoom presentations from my bedroom. Nevertheless, I still got to hear lots of interesting talks and meet other researchers (or at least their avatars). I presented our paper during the Image Synthesis session at the conference. You can check out more details and results from our work on the project webpage.
