I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science and Statistics at Trinity College Dublin since September 2020.

In 2014 I graduated from Trinity College Dublin with a First Class Honours degree in Mathematics. The following year I completed Part III of the Mathematical Tripos at the University of Cambridge. I then spent three and a half years working as an Actuarial Analyst, during which time I qualified as an Associate of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries.
